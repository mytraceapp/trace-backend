Feels like a therapist guiding them toward insight

"what surprised you?" (if asking at all)

5. "Feeling proud is important. Acknowledging your achievement can boost your confidence moving forward."
This is the worst offender. Pure therapy-speak.
Why it's terrible:

"Feeling proud is important" = therapist validating emotions
"Acknowledging your achievement can boost your confidence" = self-help book language
"moving forward" = coaching phrase

Should be:

"yeah, be proud." or "you should be." or just "damn right."
One sentence. No explanation of why pride is important.
